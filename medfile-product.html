Simple Setup, Lifesaving Results

Get started with MedFile in just a few easy steps

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up via our app or website and enter your basic information, medical history, and any allergies or current medications.

2

Get Your QR Code

We generate a unique QR code that securely contains all your essential medical information in an encrypted format.

3

Scan at Hospital

Present your QR code (digital or printed) at any participating hospital - they'll scan it to instantly access your details.
